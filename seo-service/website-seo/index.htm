Frequently Asked Questions

SEO (search engine optimization) is the practice of optimizing content to improve organic rankings on Google. As a website climbs SERPs (search engine result pages), it attracts high-quality organic traffic and forms a wide audience base.

As the most effective digital marketing strategy out there, SEO helps businesses establish a winning online presence and boost sales. With online competition becoming increasingly cut-throat each day, standing out from the crowd has become imperative. SEO helps businesses do just that by appearing in the top search results on Google.

And why does that matter?

Well, the first five organic results on Google amass over 67.6% of all clicks. If your business isn’t actively climbing SERPs, it’ll disappear among the pool of content that goes by unclicked every day.

And while ranking high on Google may sound easy, it’s not exactly a walk in the park. Google’s search algorithm uses over 200 unique factors to rank websites. Optimizing your content accordingly is thus essential, even though it can be difficult.

This is where SEO services step in!

Before understanding how SEO works, it’s important to grasp how search engines work. Search engines have three essential functions:

  • Crawling:Scouring the internet for content
  • Indexing:Sifting through, organizing, and storing content
  • Ranking:Retrieving and displaying the most relevant results in response to a searcher’s query

SEO helps search engines effectively crawl, index, and rank particular webpages. This is a great way to ensure your website remains relevant and actively appears each time a web user runs an industry-specific query.

As Google fetches your page and displays it among the top search results, you’ll generate more leads and boost sales.

Simply put, SEO increases your brand’s visibility on the web. At a time when digital marketing has become so widespread, simply creating content isn’t enough. You have to go the extra mile and optimize it for search engines to maximize impressions, clicks, and conversions by leveraging SEO.

SEO has become a necessity for businesses in 2020. By helping you climb SERPs and attract high-quality traffic, SEO ensures your business stands out from the crowd.

There are currently over 2 billion active websites on the internet. And that’s not all; the U.S. has a whopping 876.8 million active websites. By optimizing your website and web content for search engines, you automatically give your business a much-needed edge in your respective industry. This translates into better sales, increased audience engagement and retention, and a winning brand identity.

Whether you recently launched a start-up, run a small business, or own a large company, leveraging SEO is a great way to help your business dominate the digital landscape.
