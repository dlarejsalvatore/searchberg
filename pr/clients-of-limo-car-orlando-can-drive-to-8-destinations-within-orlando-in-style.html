Clients Of Limo Car Orlando Can Drive To 8 Destinations Within Orlando, In Style!
Now tourists can visit SeaWorld and other Orlando destinations easily and within their budget by hiring Limo Car Orlando!

BriefingWire.com, 11/22/2016 - Orlando, Florida – About 46.9 million Americans travel for Thanksgiving every year, all with different reasons. Keeping in-line with the holiday season, Limo Car Orlando is now offering choice of 8 destinations to travelers within the city. Of course this convenience will also be offered throughout the year, i.e. after the holiday season.

Some travel to visit their family out of the city or some distance from their homes. Others travel to Orlando and make the most of the pleasant winter and festive season. The city famous for housing Walt Disneyworld is also most known for other popular attractions, frequented throughout the year and especially during holidays. Clients have a choice of 8 destinations in total, throughout Orlando including the Orlando Airport.

“Limo Car Orlando is amongst few car rental services in Orlando that provide transport from and to the Orlando Airport as well as several locations in the city. Couples planning their wedding will see how budge-friendly our limo car rental services can be, especially when it comes to renting by the hour. Travelers to Orlando can now have a classic sedan waiting to pick up from the airport. It’s an incredible idea that offers convenience to clients when it comes to visiting Orlando,” said a spokesperson for the limo car rental company.

Besides offering timely pickup and drop services from the airport to the hotel, Limo Car Orlando provides transportation to Universal Studios, SeaWorld, Walt Disneyworld, Port Canaveral, The Mall at Millennia, night clubs at Church Street, and Lego Land. Transportation to other Orlando destinations and lesser known attractions are in the works as well.

“We are growing as a company, meaning soon Limo Car Orlando will be providing transportation services to clients for lesser know attractions and destinations. For now the popular and current destinations are highlighted, which means Limo Car Orlando offers this service while taking care of comfort and convenience of clients. Not mention, our reliable professional chauffeurs provide a luxurious experience,” continued the spokesperson.

With a simple limo car booking form, reserving a classy limo from their 2014-2015 limo fleet models has now become easier. Limo Car Orlando caters to businesses, weddings and proms too.
