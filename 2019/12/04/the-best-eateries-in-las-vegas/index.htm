The Best Eateries in Las Vegas

Posted bylvcexoticrentalsDecember 4, 2019Posted inLuxury Car Rentals

While it’s primarily known for being one of the world’s premier gambling destinations, Las Vegas also houses some of the best eateries in the United States. Sin City offers a selection of restaurants that are as diverse, glamorous, and vibrant as the Strip itself.

So if you’ve had your fill of the beautiful sights and grand casinos, and just want to enjoy a quiet candle-lit dinner with you partner, grab the keys to your dream exotic rental cars and head to any of the amazing restaurants listed below.

Joël Robuchon

Located within the world-famous MGM Grand, this is the first venture of legendary chef, Joel Robuchon, in the United States. It was this very restaurant that started an empire which consists of restaurants all over the country and earned Robuchon an astonishing 20 Michelin stars across the globe.

Featuring a beautiful setting that reminiscent of 1930’s Paris and a menu full of mouthwatering traditional French cuisine, the restaurant offers its guests the chance to taste the creations of one of the world’s most celebrated culinary icons. The walls of this magnificent restaurant are adorned by photos of Robuchon and his celebrity diners, and you can rest assured you’ll be in excellent company at Forbes 5-star rated venue.

SW Steakhouse

If you’re looking for a perfect combination of delicious food and dazzling entertainment in Las Vegas, the SW Steakhouse offers it in spades. A venture of celebrated chef, David Walzog, this restaurant is situated in the famous Wynn, and is widely considered as the best steakhouse Las Vegas has to offer.

You not only get the chance to enjoy some delicious entrees like dry-aged tomahawk chops and A5 Japanese Wagyu, but you’ll also be treated to dazzling night shows as the restaurant is located right by the Lake of Dreams.

Picasso

There’s a reason why this restaurant is renamed Picasso. It’s inspired by the regional cuisine of Spain and France – places where legendary artist, Pablo Picasso, spent most of his life. Headed by executive Chef Julian Serrano, the place offers a wide selection of amazing dishes for guests to enjoy.

Picasso also boasts a vast private cellar which has over 1,500 wines from every corner of the world. Surrounding by charming ceramics and a collection of Picasso’s original works, you’ll have a dining experience that you’ll remember for the rest of your life.
