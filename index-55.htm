Difference Between Pressure Wash vs. Soft Washing Your Home

Simply put, pressure washing is for harder surfaces that can withstand high psi water pressure without damage and it's perfect for cleaning concrete, brick, and stone. Soft washing uses much lower pressure and employs special biodegradable cleaning solutions to dissolve and remove dirt and contaminants from damage-susceptible surfaces like roofing shingles, vinyl siding, and gutters.
