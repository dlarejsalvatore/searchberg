04/15/2020: As the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic continues to spread across the globe, the large majority of Americans are struggling to combat escalating stress and anxiety levels. The pandemic has physically and psychologically affected millions of lives.
During this period of uncertainty, Steve Gilliland’s inspirational and uplifting books and keynote speeches have brought many homebound readers and viewers comfort and motivation.
Steve’s audiobooks and videos—especially Detour—are laden with inspiration, humor, energy and motivation that help audiences feel empowered and resilient as the debilitating COVID-19 crisis continues. Detour focuses on overcoming adversity and feeling more in control when life undergoes massive reorganizations that make us feel helpless.
By helping audiences build resilience and grit, Steve helps readers and viewers eliminate fear, reduce stress and navigate life’s challenging turns with their positivity and determination intact.
Steve Gilliland offers an extensive collection of e-books, audiobooks, physical products and keynote speeches to ensure people everywhere can gain easy access to his work.
This Hall of Fame speaker’s YouTube channel includes clips from his many insightful and empowering speeches. Audiences are especially impressed by his timely wit, candor and revitalizing energy, which helps people feel like they can get through bad times.
“The pandemic has caused a global mental and emotional standstill,” Steve says. “People are so overwhelmed that they can’t seem to find the courage or path to push through. By helping audiences search for and hold onto that glimmer of hope, I try to reignite a spark of perseverance and tenacity.”
Steve also offers a 4-CD collection titled A Treasury of Motivation, which offers insights into how to deal with change.

About Steve Gilliland
As one of the leading Hall of Fame speakers in North America, Steve Gilliland has earned a stellar reputation owing to his motivational and inspirational keynote speeches, presentations, books, and other products. By presenting to over 250,000 people a year, Steve has interacted with over two million people. His empowering, authentic and heartwarming style continues to make audiences feel compelled to revisit his powerful seminars time and time again.
