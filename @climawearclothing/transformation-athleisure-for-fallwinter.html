The Transformation of Athleisure for Fall/Winter 2019


Since activewear has been so popular over the past couple of years, athleisure companies come out with new pieces all the time. 2019 has seen many new and interesting trends that pay homage to the 90s. However, now that the season has changed and the temperatures have dropped, the activewear trends have also seen a transformation.

The experts at Climawear have put together some of the hottest pieces for fall and winter 2019:

High-neck sports bras

Sports bras are an essential part of an active wear wardrobe. They’re the perfect alternative to uncomfortable underwire bras. Sports bras provide optimal support while being lightweight. You can go about your whole day with one on.

This season, high-neck sports bras are in fashion. They provide more coverage and are ideal for the colder months. High-neck sports bras also provide more support than sports bras with low necklines and thin straps. Check out Climawear’s collection of medium-impact sports bras.

Matching sets

Matching sets have become all the rage. They’re being incorporated into skirts and tops as well as activewear. Whether you choose to wear capris, leggings, or shorts, make sure they are paired with a similar top.

Sustainability

As more and more people realize the impact of their choices on the earth’s environment, the more the efforts of repairing the damage can be seen. Individuals choose eco-friendly products over products that add to environmental degradation.

The average American contributes 82 pounds of textile waste every year. To help combat this issue of textile waste and to reduce carbon emissions, activewear companies like Climawear offer eco-friendly products that ensure 40% less waste.

Tie-dye pieces

The trend of paying homage to the 90s is still going strong. Tie-die pieces have made a major comeback, even in the world of activewear. Tie-die leggings and tops can easily be found at Climawear.

Seamless activewear pieces

Seamless activewear takes the comfort of the athleisure trend to a whole other level. You won’t feel the seams of your leggings digging into your skin nor have any indents on your skin after wearing activewear pieces for a long time. Seamless technology with 360° stretch adds durability and comfort to leggings, sports bras, and tops. Climawear specializes in seamless activewear pieces.

Climawear is an online store that provides top-of-the-line premium activewearpieces for women. Their goal is to help and motivate women to lead a happy and healthy lifestyle. Their functional and stylish pieces aim to boost a woman’s confidence and celebrate her figure. The company provides seamless leggings, long-sleeve tops, medium, and low impact sport bras for sale at affordable prices.
