Three Million Business Stories. Whats Yours?

Play Icon
Update: The opportunity to create Your Business Story has now ended. Thank you to all the businesses that participated.
Today, three million businesses actively advertise on Facebook. Thats three million companies from all over the world, with more than 70% from outside of the US.
Each of these businesses has a unique story and mission. Whether its fashion or fitness, confidence or creativity, each business offers something to its customers and communities that no one else does.
To celebrate the businesses that use Facebook to grow, we created Your Business Storya tool that makes it easy to create a video that shows what your business brings to the world. Because we believe the best way to tell the story of three million businesses is to empower each one to share their own.
Play Icon
These are just some of the many stories behind the businesses on Facebook:
Brazilian music producer, Fernando Christoff, was inspired by his life as a DJ to create an exclusive clothing line for people in the music industry.
Dirty Feet, an organization in Secunderabad, India, brings urban children on weekend trips to experience and connect with nature.
The German company, Holzconnection, brings the traditional craft of cabinet-making into the 21st century through an online store and Facebook Page.
Cupcakin Bake Shop, once a home-based cupcake catering business, is now a full retail shop in Berkeley, California with 15 employees.
-
At Facebook, were in the business of helping your businesses grow. Wed like to give a big thanks to you and the millions of other advertisers who believe in us to do just that.
Join us in celebrating the millions of businesses around the world that are using Facebook to grow their business by sharing your business story with the world.
