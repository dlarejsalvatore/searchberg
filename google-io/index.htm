Google I/O Live Blog: Home voice-activated assistant, Allo messaging app announced

Google announces its rival to the Amazon Echo, unveils yet another messaging app.

Danny Sullivan on May 18, 2016 at 12:45 pm

Today at Google I/O, Google’s annual developers conference, Google announced its new Home voice-activated household assistant, a new Allo messaging app, unveiled a new “Google assistant” platform along with other developments.

We’ll have break out stories on some of the news, which you’ll find below as it appears:

  • Meet Google assistant: A new search platform, rather than a gadget or an app
  • Make room for Google Home, Amazon Echo/Alexa
  • Google announces Firebase Analytics & Universal App Campaigns updates for app developers
