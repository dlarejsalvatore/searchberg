Frequently Asked Questions

SEO keywords are terms and phrases that are incorporated in web content to improve its search engine rankings. A website with a strong ratio of SEO keywords is more likely to appear in the top search results on Google.

Keywords are determined based on the kind of content users are looking for, i.e. their search queries on Google. For instance, if you run a hair salon, your industry-specific SEO keywords will include “best hair salons near me,” “hair stylist,” and so on. It’s important to note that keywords are constantly changing, which makes active and thorough SEO keyword research imperative.

Keywords help businesses rank higher on Google. As one of the most effective SEO strategies, keyword research and implementation ensures a business attracts high-quality and high-volume traffic.

Our SEO specialists utilize cutting-edge tools and resources to find and analyze high-ranking keywords. They actively monitor Google’s algorithm updates, relevant topics, and the latest trends to ensure optimal keyword usage. In addition, they check how the client’s competitors are ranking for specific keywords. These keyword research strategies (among several others) play a big role in helping our clients stand out from the crowd and consistently rank high on Google.

The right number of SEO keyword incorporation depends on a wide range of factors, including the length, form, and goal of the content. Ideally, we recommend using the target keyword once or twice per 100 words. Of course, this largely varies. At Search Berg, we do the work for you so you don’t have to worry about the nitty-gritty of keyword usage.

A good keyword helps a website rank higher on Google and earn high-quality traffic. At Search Berg, we ensure our target keywords meet certain criteria—including search intent, search volume, relevance, conversion value, and competition. The outcome? Improved lead generation and powerful sales.
