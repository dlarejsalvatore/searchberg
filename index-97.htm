    Industries we serve

    Sorbead India caters to the needs of many industries. The major industries include Agriculture, Architectural materials, Automotive, Aerospace and Aviation, Chemicals, Coatings, Consumer products, Cosmetics & personal care, Computers, Defence industry, Electronics, Food and Beverages, Machine Parts, Medical Instruments, Military, Natural Gas Processing, Packaging, Petroleum refining, Photo Equipment, Pharmaceuticals, Power generation, Public Utilities, Refrigeration, Semiconductor, Shipping and Storage, Water filtration among many others.

    Let us now take a closer look at some of these industries and how Sorbead India works for them:

    Pharmaceuticals:

    In the pharmaceutical industry, products like Foam Plugs, Tubes & Vials, oxygen absorbers, true desiccant pouches & Canisters for protecting tablets, capsules packed inside in bottles from the ill-effects of moisture. These desiccants are also used in packaging of Nutraceuticals, cosmetics and natural organic products.

    Oil and Natural Gas:

    Adsorbents and desiccants manufactured by Sorbead India find great use in oil and natural gas industries, where they are used for the separation and purification of oil and natural gases. They mostly use Silica Gel, Molecular sieves & Activated Alumina Desiccants.

    Research Laboratories:

    Research labs make extensive use of chromatography substances for drying of analytical representatives, DNA purification & biomarkers and most importantly, column chromatography for the isolation and clean-up of antibiotics.

    Chemical Industry:

    Sorbead India manufactures Alumina balls for the drying of organic liquids like LPG, butane, steam cracked liquids and aromatic solvents.

    Shipping Industry:

    Adsorbents like Cargo Dry Pak are extensively used in the shipping industry for the safe and secure packaging of goods meant for ocean, truck, and rail and air movement.

    Food Industry:

    Oxygen absorber from Sorbead India helps food industry to maintain product freshness and extend shelf life of packaged foods. According to food standard oxygen absorber help to decrease or remove oxygen level from the food package.
