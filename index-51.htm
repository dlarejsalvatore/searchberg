Welcome to The Estes Law Firm

FORT BEND PERSONAL INJURY ATTORNEYS LOCATED IN RICHMOND, TEXAS

Dealing with an injury is undeniably difficult. Not only do you have to worry about recovery, but you may also be concerned about your inability to work and provide for your family. If you’ve been injured in an accident because of another’s negligence, you may be entitled to compensation. The team at The Estes Law Firm is here to help you navigate Texas law and receive a fair settlement for your expenses and pain and suffering. Learn more about what our experienced attorneys can offer you.

Get the Compensation You Need

Whether you’re up against another individual or an entire trucking company, our team can help. Over the years, our attorneys have worked with clients to take on major companies and their insurance providers. We’re dedicated to getting you the compensation you need to make your recovery a little easier. Talk to our team about the potential damages you may receive as a part of your settlement. You may be entitled to compensation covering property damage, lost wages, hospital bills, and more.

Take a Look at Our Legal Practice Areas

Each of our attorneys has years of experience working with Richmond, TX clients. In that time, we’re gained a breadth of knowledge in a variety of practice areas. Whatever you need, trust that our attorneys can accommodate you. Here are just a few categories our specialties fall into:

  • Car, Motorcycle, and Truck Accidents: We work with community members impacted by a motor vehicle accident. Our team can handle car accidents as well as 18-wheeler accidents.
  • Personal Injury Claims: Our lawyers will work closely with you to build an airtight personal injury In years past, we’ve handled dog bites, brain injuries, drunk driver accidents, and much more.
  • Wrongful Death: If a loved one has passed away because of another’s negligent behavior, you and your family may be entitled to compensation. Sit down with us to discuss your circumstances, and we’ll develop a course of action.
  • Labor and Employment: We are also glad to work with clients in cases of workplace injury, unpaid overtime, and other claims associated with employment.
