HISTORY

In 1994, Kleen Office Environments (KOE) was built on a foundation of hard work, integrity, and a passion for serving the West Michigan area. With over 20 years in the professional cleaning industry, KOE has maintained these values along with a commitment to continuous training and improvement to provide the highest quality service and customer satisfaction. Our approach to creating a clean, healthy, and attractive environment is always customized to meet the needs of each client.

COMMITMENT

At KOE, we pride ourselves on open, honest communication and we truly value the relationships we build with each of our clients. We hold ourselves accountable to be reliable, responsive, and professional so you can maintain business as usual. Our trained staff is committed to providing a positive experience and implements the ideal combination of proven procedures and advanced cleaning technology to acheive the results our clients deserve.

MISSION STATEMENT

Kleen Office Environments' mission is to provide detailed, quality cleaning services to commercial facilities at a reasonable cost and to foster open communication with each client to tailor our services to fit each company's specific needs.
