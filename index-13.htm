Our Mission: To motivate and continuously challenge every child to be their best; guiding, engaging, and supporting each child in academics and enrichment activities that build intellectual growth, physical development, and authentic confidence in learning.

From toddler programs, to preschool, to kindergarten, and even after school programs, we are here to support children and families grow. 

Our programs provide children with early academic exposure to create a lifelong love of learning. Students in our preschool and kindergarten program start with English, math, music, arts, social science and life science. We strongly believe that early and age-appropriate exposure to arts and academics develops a strong foundation of understanding and learning. At Little Scholar, we prepare children to excel academically and creatively and to develop social emotional skills. Our research-based, multi-sensory curriculum is based on the Orton-Gillingham model.
