Frequently Asked Questions

Citation building is the process of sharing relevant and accurate information about your business with local directories and reputable websites. This is a great way to boost rankings and gain greater online visibility and credibility. As your business gathers mentions, you’ll become an authoritative voice in the industry and gain an edge over your competition.

A local citation is any mention of the name, address, phone number, or website URL of a business online. Citations are commonly created on websites, apps, local business directories, and social media platforms.

Citation building informs Google that your website is a legitimate source in the industry. This, in turn, leads to your website being ranked higher.

If you’re interested in improving your search engine result page (SERP) rankings and increasing quality traffic, citation building services are a great option to look into. The more accurate and relevant citations your business gathers, the better the chances you have of ranking high on Google’s SERPs.
