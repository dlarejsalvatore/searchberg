The latest information

Deliveries, returns and Lacoste stores

Dear Crocodiles,


We hope you are healthy and safe.

Following the latest government announcements, we inform you on temporary adjustments to some of our in-store services, deliveries and the loyalty programme.


Also be aware that opening hours may change during the different phases of lock down and tiers in the UK, see them here. For the protection of all, in each store we continue to meet the standards needed under this current context and look forward to welcoming you where we are open with the greatest care.


We look forward to seeing you again and giving you the best possible experience.


See you very soon dear Crocodiles!

ANSWERS TO YOUR QUESTIONS

What are the key delivery updates to receive orders?

Delivery is £10 on orders under £35 and £5.95 on orders between £35 and £80.

We deliver using DHL Express and takes up to 2-3 working days between Monday to Friday.


Please be assured that all orders placed will not incur duty fees & additional charges.


We aim to provide a fast and secure delivery at each step for you to enjoy your order in good time.

Can I make a return to one of your stores?

Our stores will assist you with your online return. Please refer to our store locator to check opening and closing of your local store.

Can I still order using Click & Collect?

We have temporarily removed the option to collect your items in our boutiques. We will update you as soon as we are able to offer this service again.

How can I redeem my loyalty gift?

Your welcome gift and birthday gifts will only be redeemable in store at one of our boutiques which are open. Loyalty gifts are temporarily unavailable online. We apologise for any inconvenience and will update soon.

What stores are open and what are the sanitation procedures?

Please check our store locator to find your local store information and opening hours. If a store is closed you will also find this information here. Find your local store.


Please note every care is taken in our stores to follow sanitation procedures throughout the day.

The wearing of the mask is highly recommended for all our team and hand sanitiser will be offered to you.
Your store is disinfected many times during the day while payment terminals are disinfected before and after each use.
Our staff are respecting a social distance of 2 metres with our customers. The number of people inside the shop is limited.
In order to preserve the safety of all, card payment is recommended in the store.

We are here for you

For all other questions, our customer service will be very happy to assist you.

  • Free returns

    Free returns are accepted 30 days after receipt of your order. You can do this for free using the return label accessed under your account or via the order shipment email.

    Once you declare your return request you will receive an email with your return label. This will include two invoices plus a DHL waybill label which should be given to DHL upon pick up or drop off of your box. Please re-use the box in which the order was delivered and close your parcel securely.

    Refunds will be made back to the original payment method. Please note, customised items, masks and underwear are not eligible for return.

  • WE KEEP YOUR DETAILS SAFE

    All payment information is protected by secure connection to ensure your card data cannot be used by anyone else.

    The following payment methods are available at the Lacoste online shop:
    VISA, MASTERCARD, AMERICAN EXPRESS AND PAYPAL

  • Free Standard Delivery - on orders over £80

    Standard delivery is free on orders over £80.

    Standard delivery is £5,95 and takes 4-5 working days from Monday to Friday and is offered on orders over £80.

    Express delivery is £9,95 and takes takes 1-2 working days from Monday to Friday.

    Delivery in store or to a pick up station : temporarily deactivated.

    Please be assured that all orders placed either on standard or express delivery will not incur duty fees and additional charges.
