Five Star Hardwood Flooring Offers Safe Dustless Hardwood Refinishing That Protects the Wellbeing of Clients in Kirkland

Posted January 6, 2020 by fivestarwood

The company caters to a niche in the interior design industry by offering services that are known for quality and customer care, such as dustless refinishing.

Five Star Hardwood Floor has secured an unparalleled reputation in the industry over the past decade with their undeterred commitment to quality and care. Their services are not only rendered with precision and expertise but also with conscientious compliance with health standards. Dustless hardwood refinishing is a prime example of this. This not only ensures that the job is done with minimum waste but also shows great concern for the safety of people on site. Their name is popular in Kirkland and many other cities of Washington as well.
The company’s refinishing technique ensures that there are no airborne dust particles left on the upholstery and furniture. This minimizes the cost and time of covering all exposed surfaces with plastic sheets that are required to prevent damage otherwise. But with dustless refinishing, there’s no such risk. The ducts also remain clear of any collected dust, significantly minimizing the risks of damage to the HVAC system. This prioritizes the safety and convenience of the clients who no longer have to worry about evacuating the place and finding alternate lodging for the meanwhile.
With a sanding system as effective, time-efficient, and waste-free, Five Star Hardwood Flooring undoubtedly reigning supreme in the local market. For clients who are concerned about the results of this service, their website attests to have refinished 60-year old flooring with excellent results.
A spokesperson of the company said: “We understand that homeowners find it difficult to move out of their place when it’s time for a floor restoration. It’s not easy to find an alternate place for the time being and certainly not convenient to pay for it, on top of service charges. The effort doubles when you have a big family to travel with. It just means as many bags to pack and needs to look after, while the floor is being redone. We don’t want to put our clients through any such trouble, or any inconvenience for that matter. This is why we pride ourselves on our dustless refinishing technique, which ensures that the procedure is carried out without causing any harm to the inmates or to the surroundings. Your safety and comfort is our top-most priority!”

Refinishing is a fundamental service that they specialize in but they cover many more bases in interior designing and restoration. Their custom stain colors are known among the local community for the scale of options it offers, and has thus become popular on demand. This has allowed residential and commercial clients to have a freehand in working with the theme they have in mind and pick a color for the floor accordingly. It also makes it possible for clients to renew the look of their place with minor touchups and finishing. Their Bona Traffic Floor Finish is spectacularly resistant to wear and offers flawless durability even in high-traffic areas.
Their services are highly sought after in Kirkland for many more reasons than are listed above, and especially because they offer in-home consultations free of cost.

About The Company

Five Star Hardwood Floor specializes in hardwood floor installation and repairs as well as wall and ceiling design services. At present, the company caters to a number of cities in Washington including Kirkland and other regions.
