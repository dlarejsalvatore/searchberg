Welcome to Advanced Dental Center

Advanced Dental Center takes pride in serving its customers with comprehensive and high quality care in a casual setting. Serving families and children with over 15 years of excellence, exceptional care, and quality dental treatment, we are the most reliable dental care facility in Germantown. We specialize in providing exceptional individual care when it comes to dental procedures, and make your experience comfortable and resourceful every time you visit our facility.

We provide general dental care for both children and adults including cleanings, fillings, crowns, bridges, dentures, and gum treatment. Our dentists also offer a full range of cosmetic treatments, from teeth whitening to veneers, to help you attain the beautiful and confident smile you have always wanted and deserved. Our office is conveniently located in Germantown on the corner of Crystal Rock Drive and Middlebrook Road, adjacent to Seneca Valley High School. We offer morning, evening, and weekend hours to accommodate your schedule. Please contact our office to schedule an appointment today. We can also help answer any questions you may have. Providing you with the smile you deserve!

A healthy smile is a great gift. Your mouth is the gateway to your entire body, which means that regular oral care is necessary, not only to give you a confident smile, but also to prevent any lasting health issues.

This is why we offer excellent professional dental services. From general dentistry, including complete checkups and fluoride treatments for adults, to cosmetic dentistry, including veneers and teeth whitening, we offer the various dental care solutions.

Our practice was built with the goals of giving each patient a satisfying dental experience. We have dedicated dental experts, specializing in a wide range of preventative and restoration treatments with the highest standard of patient care.
