Local SEO (search engine optimization) marketing is an effective way to market a local business online. As your online presence is locally optimized, your business will attract high-quality traffic from relevant local searches. It’s no surprise that a whopping 46% of all Google searches are for local businesses. Simply put, local SEO marketing helps your business become visible to this large percentage of local searchers.

Local SEO works by boosting a site’s Google ranking for local search. This is achieved by optimizing the website, web content, business listings, and social media pages.

Our local SEO experts use a wide range of effective and actionable strategies to achieve quick and consistent results. These strategies help your business appear in the top local search results on Google.

Local SEO is a whip-smart way to tweak your online presence in accordance with the unique set of ranking factors used by Google. As a result, your business will generate more local leads and maximize sales.

SEO is the process of increasing the quantity and quality of organic traffic to your website. Local SEO is a subset of SEO that strictly focuses on local search.

For instance, leveraging SEO is a great way to help your business appear in the top search results each time a web user runs a generic search. However, this may not be of value to your business if you only operate in a certain location.

Local SEO makes things more specific so you can target potential customers in your geographic area. This is extremely beneficial for businesses that offer their products/services in a certain town, city, state, or country. Instead of reaching a global audience that cannot invest in your products/services, you can tap into a wide local audience that contributes to your revenue.

However, local businesses shouldn’t sideline vanilla SEO entirely. We suggest using both SEO and local SEO strategies to build a strong online reputation and generate high-quality local leads.

If your business operates within a certain geographic region, we suggest opting for local SEO services.

Even if you don’t have a local target market, local SEO can help you generate more leads in certain popular cities, towns, or states. This is a great way to create a powerful online presence for locations that contribute to your revenue more than others.
