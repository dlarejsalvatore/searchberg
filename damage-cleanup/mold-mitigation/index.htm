Mold Remediation Services

Mold, a fungus that is found in damp places, can grow almost anywhere moist conditions exist. Left unattended, it can cause health problems as well as serious structural damage to your facility. Mold Mitigation Services

Any areas in the house that have damp conditions are prone to developing mold and fungus. If ignored, the mold and fungus will multiply exponentially and pose a health risk.

We eliminate mold and fungus using a fool-proof systematic approach. Our team identifies the source of the mold and removes it immediately. We then inspect all areas of the premises to clean up the mold and fungus present in the building. Count on us to get every nook and cranny!

Mold cleanups involve the following:

  • Locating all the mold and fungus present in the building, assessing the damage and taking necessary steps to clean it up.
  • We use negative air pressure as well as physical barriers to stop the mold from spreading.
  • Eliminating microscopic spores of mold from the air.
  • Using antifungal and antimicrobial treatments.
