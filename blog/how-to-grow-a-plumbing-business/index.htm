2. Google Places

Plumbing is a local business, limited to certain locations of a city. It’s important to ensure that your company is exposed to the potential clients of the location(s) you serve, which may include Fort Worth, Austin, Houston, Los Angeles, Kansas City and so on.

To add your plumbing firm to Google Places, take the following steps:

  • Visit Google Places
  • Click “start now”
  • Fill in the form with your business information
  • Choose your service areas
  • Mention your working hours and days
  • Select payment options
  • Upload company photos
  • Attach YouTube videos if you wish
  • Add more available business details
  • Double-check the information you entered and click “verify”

That was easy, wasn’t it?

Google Places shows a special listing representing only three local companies.

Now imagine how many plumbers are already working in your area and how many have already added their business to Google Places!
