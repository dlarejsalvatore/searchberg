Artisan Excursion Offers All-Inclusive Dinners To Their Wine Tour Clients In Solvang

The California-based company makes sure their clients end their Solvang wine tours with a luxurious dinner party

Solvang, California—04-16-2020: Artisan Excursion Wine Tours, a CA-based wine tourism company, offers wine tours to tourists, vacationers, and locals in Solvang, CA along with all-inclusive luxurious dinners to complete the experience.
The company was founded in 1976 by Tom and has been operating locally for the past 13 years in the wine valley. Their wine tours are a popular tourist attraction as it familiarizes guests with some of the best wines available. The company also offers customers trips to other important landmarks like historical places, cultural centers and different adventure sites via private transportation mediums.
Ever since the company was started, it has been committed to making sure that their guests have the time of their lives and leave for home feeling refreshed and relaxed. Artisan Excursion Wine Tours maintain their business standards by offering clients some of the most exquisite wine labels.
The company offers wine tours in small groups catering to up to 10 people at a time for a price of $129 per person. The tour includes visits to different wine trails and even private vineyards with a picnic lunch included in the package. Guests also have the choice to opt for their ‘group dinner in the vine’ pairing by offering them an exclusive dinner in a private vineyard with a 5-star executive chef.
“Our clients come to us to take a break from their busy lives. These are people with high-pressure jobs who are taking a vacation to de-stress and take some time to relax and rejuvenate. So, we make it a point to offer them an experience that will clear their minds like nothing else. We offer them the finest wines and a visually soulful experience at one of the finest places in California. We make sure that our dinners are an uplifting farm to table culinary experience, including 5-course meals prepared by a local executive”, a spokesperson for the company recently commented in a conference while talking about the kind of experience the company aims to offer to their clients.
Artisan Excursion Wine Tours intends to make the over-all wine and tour experience for their customers the best possible including offering private transport for up to 10 people for $75 per hour.
They also offer a no-cancellation fee policy if the client cancels seven days before the departure. If the client cancels four to five days prior, a 50% of the original fee will be applicable, and if the cancellation is made three days before departure, a 100% cancellation fee will be applied.

About Artisan Excursion Wine Tours
Artisan Excursion Wine Tours offers vacationers a chance to experience the essence of Santa Ynez Valley by providing guided wine tours. They take visitors to some of California’s most notable wineries, such as SolvangLompoc, Santa Ynez, Buellton, Los Olivos, and Los Alamos. They also offer meal arrangements in the open with an intimate experience of the vast fields and dense vineyards.
