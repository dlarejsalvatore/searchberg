    ABOUT US

    We are your one stop destination for delicious Indian Food in Seattle

    We are an award-winning family restaurant beautifully located in Seattle WA. We have been serving finger-licking authentic Indian food in our restaurants and satisfying the cravings of our local customers and tourists from all over the world for over a decade. Once you try our delicious Indian meals, we are certain that you will fall in love with our food over and over again. All your favorites such as Chicken Tikka Masala, Biryani, Garlic Naan, Goat Curry, etc. we have all of that in our menu also we have a large selection of Indian dishes along with local and imported beverage choices to quench your thirst.

     

    You can even order our Indian food online in Seattle, WA. Simply choose your preferred time and method of delivery (we also give you the option of picking up the food from the restaurant), and authentic Indian cuisine will arrive at your doorstep!

    Executive Head Chef

    Gurmohan Singh Sehra 

    Mr. Singh, owner and head chef of India Bistro in Seattle, Washington, has been cooking for as long as he has been in America. He loves creating new and exciting dishes from various authentic Indian recipes he gathers.


    Cooking is not a job for him, but a hobby. His true passion is to see a smile on his guests face after they taste and experience his delicious Indian meals.

    India Bistro takes pride in creating fresh, healthy and nutritious Indian food. Freshness is ingrained in every dish that is cooked to the taste of our guests, fresh spices are blended to bring a mouthwatering taste.

    Whether you are on a family outing, partying with friends, going out for dinner with your client or want to celebrate a party with family and friends, come to India Bistro and enjoy the Real Taste of India. You can even choose to order our Indian food online and bring the India Bistro to your home in Seattle!
