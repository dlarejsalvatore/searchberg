      Training For Mental Health Experts

      The field of psychology and counseling is continuously evolving. As mental health counselors, therapists, social workers, and psychologists create, learn, and implement new techniques used for intervention, they contribute to the plethora of existing knowledge.

      It’s imperative that trained and professional mental health experts don’t lose sight of what’s new and are kept updated with the recent, most effective concepts and practices.

      At E Care Behavioral Institute, we provide continuing education for all mental health professionals to help them get the additional training they need. We offer online workshops and seminars through our live interactive and recorded webinars, making education even more accessible to our students. In addition to providing training for peer support specialists and professional counselors, we also offer CEUs for LMFT.

      Our economical courses and training programs don’t compromise on the quality of education being offered. In fact, with our state-of-the-art educational trainings, we help professional mental health experts further polish their skills and enhance their knowledge.

      To learn more about our work, get in touch with our team by calling at 704 883 6615 or drop us an email on info@ecarebehavioralinstitute.com.
