The Brief Guide To Wedding Planning


Dresses, drinks and dancing – the ‘I dos’ of holy matrimony deserve special attention. Before you get clad in the elegant white of your gown, there is a lot of planning to do!

Though a wedding has to have a unique essence of the couple’s spirit, there are some common details in every ceremony.

Catch up on the wedding planning basics for your big day!

Talk out the planning

Once you get a ring on your finger, you start dreaming of a million different scenarios in which you will say your vows. Convey your ideas to your fiancé to find a common ground for venue and dates.
· You and your soon to be significant other, and your families, will have to come together to sort out a guest list. Your wedding’s size is a crucial factor in deciding whether your favored venue will hold the entire wedding party, and how you are going to transport them.

· Leave the budget talk to the end of the magical trance. Know that you can still arrange that classy entrance and guest entertainment with careful spending, it’s a matter of finding the best deals once you figure out how much can be contributed towards the wedding.
Now that you have formed a checklist, go ahead and plan out the basics.



Venue: Choose a location that suited for the weather and is sizeable for the entire wedding party to move and dance around. If you’re opting for a favored wedding venue, then make sure to book months in advance.

Catering: Research well, and delve into past customer feedback before you choose a catering. Find one that offers exclusive personalized services. Do not hesitate to ask for a sampling of the hors d’oeuvres and entrees.

Décor: Shimmering drapes and intricate floral settings – the décor sets the mood of the wedding. Rather than going for conventional themes, customize the color palette according to your style.  

Photography/ Videography: Much like the venues, the best photographers and videographers are booked months ahead. Review their work on their online pages to match up to the style of shots you want on your big day.

Transportation: There is no better way to arrive than a sleek limousine pulling up to the venue.
Limos have spacious interiors that accommodate a large number of people (your bridal entourage) who would otherwise spend more by traveling separately.

If you’re concerned about splurging too much over a limo service, head on over to Limo Car Orlando; they offer sophisticated rides at affordable rates for wedding parties.

Wedding planning is a lot of fun, and with well-thought out arrangements, you can avoid a lot of last minute hassles.
