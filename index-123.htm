What To Expect at LifeSpan Pilates | Stretch | Gymnastics

LifeSpan Pilates is a fully equipped Pilates studio offering a wide range of classes across the core disciplines of Romana’s Pilates. As the New York City Certification Center of Romana’s Pilates, LifeSpan is deliberate with its approach to maintaining the integrity of pilates in its original form by cultivating and nurturing the community. You will be trained by the very best instructors in Romana’s Pilates, Gymnastics, and Stretch conditioning systems.

By offering a deeper, more sophisticated level of training, LifeSpan Pilates helps clients achieve balance by understanding their Powerhouse and how to use it. Romana’s Pilates Certified Instructors prepare trainers to provide clients with exemplary training derived from the original source of Romana’s Pilates.

Our classes offer a wide variety of physical activities, such as Pilates, stretching, adult gymnastics, and flexibility and mobility workouts. You can work at your own pace and gradually unlock your highest level of fitness!

Your classes are personalized and designed specifically for your personal needs and strengths. Our instructors will assess your fitness level and train you accordingly.

We push you just to the edge of your limit so that you emerge stronger than ever. Wear workout attire, bring socks and a desire to strengthen and stretch your entire body.

LifeSpan Pilates is a safe, no judgment zone—seriously! We take pride in our instruction, our community, and the respect we have for each other.
